Barn swallow

The barn swallow is a familiar and meaningful sight in Prince Edward Island, where its graceful flight and cheerful calls signal the arrival of spring and warmer days. Often seen nesting in barns and rural buildings, the bird reflects PEI’s strong agricultural roots and close connection between people and the land. Just as Islanders value tradition, resilience, and community, the barn swallow returns year after year to the same places, symbolizing continuity and home. Its presence in PEI highlights the island’s rich natural heritage and the importance of protecting the rural landscapes that both wildlife and local communities depend on.
