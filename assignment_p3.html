Sand martin

The sand martin is a small migratory bird that nests in sandy riverbanks and coastal cliffs. On Prince Edward Island, it is closely connected to the island’s sandy shores and eroding banks, where it finds ideal nesting habitats. Its presence highlights PEI’s delicate coastal environment and the importance of protecting the island’s natural landscapes.

following are some features of the sand martin

  1. Small, slender bird and the smallest of the swallows
  2. Nests in colonies, digging burrows into sandy banks and cliffs
  3. Plays an important role in controlling insect populations
  4. Brown upperparts with a white underside and a distinctive brown breast band
