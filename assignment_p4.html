Red Knot

The red knot is a long-distance migratory shorebird that relies on coastal habitats to rest and refuel during its journey. Prince Edward Island’s beaches and mudflats provide important feeding areas for the red knot, especially during migration, highlighting PEI’s role in supporting migratory birds and the need to protect its coastal ecosystems.

following are some features of the red knot

  1. Small to medium-sized migratory shorebird
  2. Notable reddish-orange breast during the breeding season
  3. Often found along coastal beaches and mudflats
  4. Strongly dependent on healthy coastal ecosystems
